## 📖 About the Company (For "About Us" Page)

PT. Agri Prima Indotama has been a trusted partner in Palm Oil Industry support since **1993**, with over **30 years of hands-on industrial experience**. The company operates out of Medan, North Sumatera, and has a sister company, PT. Agri Prima Indonesia, based in Batam.

The company specializes in delivering integrated solutions for industrial maintenance, equipment servicing, spare parts supply, and specialty chemicals — serving clients across the Palm Oil, Food & Beverage, Electronics, Healthcare, Hotel, Pulp & Paper industries, and more.

PT. Agri Prima Indotama maintains **close cooperation with worldwide companies** from Europe, USA, Japan, China, Taiwan, and Singapore, representing well-known manufacturers across a wide range of spare parts applications.

The company is committed to:
- Product quality and service excellence
- Optimizing client production efficiency
- Reducing long-term operating costs
- Creating a cleaner working environment
- Continuous Research & Development
